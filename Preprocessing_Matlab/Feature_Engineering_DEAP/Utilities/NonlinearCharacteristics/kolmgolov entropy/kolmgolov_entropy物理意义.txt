Kolmogorov熵是在相空间中刻画混沌运动的一个重要量度，它反映了系统动态过程中信息的平均损失率。

The Kolmogorov entropy measures the rate of information loss per unit time.A positive, finite entropy generally is considered to be a clear demonstration that the time series and its underlying dynamics are chaotic. A large entropy indicates a stochastic, totally unpredictable phenomenon.
